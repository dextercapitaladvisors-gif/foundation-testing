Our Vision

Empowering Through Education

Dexter Foundation is the brain child of our Founder, Devendra Agrawal, who was born in a small village of Rajasthan, Hasampur and discovered education as the single most important life-altering parameter in his life. The foundation is committed to enable rural underprivileged kids to discover education opportunities and experience a huge impact resulting from it.

Free Coaching

Navodaya Entrance Exam

The Dexter Foundation is currently training 100 students in partnership with IN-DEED Foundation from rural Rajasthan in 4 locations:
Jodhpur | Kotputli | Hasampur | Sojat

These students are being given free coaching in study centers near them by experienced teachers in Hindi, Math and Mental Ability so that they can clear the entrance exam for Navodaya Vidyalaya.

We have already delivered more than 25,000 hours of classroom training and helped students register successfully. Irrespective of results, students are exposed to a new way of life, which we hope stays with them.

English Training for Rural Students

Dexter Foundation sponsored Online and Offline English classes for 250 rural students from
Rajasthan | Himachal Pradesh | Karnataka | Odisha | Assam

These students, from weaker sections of society, were taught English from basics to improve spoken and written skills. This was delivered through the NGO EmpBindi International Association.

The program was a success, helping students in academics and giving them confidence.

Our Journey, Our People

Stories that inspire and reflect the spirit of Dexter Foundation.

My name is Manoj, from Hasampur, a small village with few educational opportunities. My father was a cloth merchant, and though I loved studying, the people around me often discouraged me, saying things like, ‘Kaunsa doctor, engineer ban jaoge?’ For a while, it felt like my dreams were too far away.

Everything changed when I entered Navodaya. The environment, discipline, and dedicated teachers opened doors I never thought possible. With their support, I prepared for CPMT, earned a good rank, and became an orthopaedic doctor. Today, I run my own hospital in Kotputli, serving my community with pride.

Navodaya not only transformed my life but also my family’s future. Inspired by my journey, both my younger brothers became engineers. The ripple effect of Navodaya uplifted us all, I will always be grateful.

As a young girl entering Navodaya in 2003, I was full of dreams but held back by my own shyness and stage fright. Seven years later, in 2010, I walked out with a confidence I never knew I had.

Navodaya didn’t just give me an education; it transformed me. I learned the power of hard work, discipline, and persistence, turning my stage fear into the confidence to win first prize in public speaking. Today, as a software engineer with a fulfilling career, I am a testament to the life-changing impact of Navodaya's holistic education.

It taught me invaluable life skills and gave me a second family. Through the Dexter Foundation, we want to help other young, bright students unlock their potential, just as Navodaya helped me unlock mine.

Navodaya has always been more than a school – it was a family. We entered its gates as children with tears in our eyes but left with lifelong bonds and memories.

The institution gave me opportunities that shaped my outlook — from sports and NCC to experiencing India’s diverse cultures. It taught me to respect different perspectives, embrace teamwork, and develop leadership skills that many gain much later in life.

The disciplined environment built focus and determination, which helped me crack IIT JEE. The culture of healthy competition, equality, and mutual respect laid a strong foundation for my academic and personal growth.

Navodaya gave me a second family and a philosophy to live life to the fullest — something I’ll always be grateful for.

Partnering for Impact

This initiative reflects our belief that access to quality education can transform lives and that every child deserves the chance to dream big.

In 2024–25, the Dexter Foundation collaborated with IN-DEED Foundation (program lead) and Multibhashi (education partner) to launch a dedicated training initiative for rural students preparing for the Navodaya Vidyalaya Entrance Exam.

108 students from 4 districts of Rajasthan enrolled in the program.

They received 250+ hours of structured training, including topic-based classes, mock tests, and OMR-based revision exams.

Parent–Teacher meetings were conducted throughout to keep families engaged and motivated.
